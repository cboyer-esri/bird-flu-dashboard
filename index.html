Avian Influenza in U.S. Wild Birds

Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza (HPAI) Detections Dashboard

Wild birds can carry highly pathogenic avian influenza (HPAI) — a severe form of bird flu — and spread it across regions during migration, often without showing symptoms. Tracking these detections helps protect domestic poultry and other wildlife.

This dashboard maps every confirmed HPAI detection in wild birds across the United States since the current outbreak began in early 2022. Filter by state, species, strain, date range, or use the lasso tool to select counties on the map.
